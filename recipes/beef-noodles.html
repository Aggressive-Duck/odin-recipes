A delicious bowl of beef noodle

Beef noodle soup is a noodle soup made of stewed or braised beef, beef broth, vegetables and noodles.

It exists in various forms throughout East and Southeast Asia.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Noodles
  • Douban
  • Spring onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Beef bones or chicken bones
  • garlic
